# Negroni


## Ingredients

Considering a general glass or recipient, divide the ingredients in the following way:

1. 1/3 part of gin
2. 1/3 part of vermouth
3. 1/3 part of campari



## Instructions

Mix and shake gently, don't drink too much of them.

5. **Serve:**
   - Serve warm and enjoy! (But be careful, the ice could melt!)
