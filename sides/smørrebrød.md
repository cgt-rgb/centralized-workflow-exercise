*** Smørrebrød

## Description
This famous Danish dish come sfrom an ancient radition of fishermen and farmers.
This is the Italian version.

## Ingredients

1. Black bread (or another type)
2. Eggs
3. Onion
4. Oil
5. Snaps

## Instructions

**1.** Boil and cut the eggs in slices
**2.** Cut the onions and cook them with a round of oil on a pan
**3.** Cut the bread in slices and roast it
**4.** Serve everything on the bread with a glass of Snaps

Enjoy your lunch!
